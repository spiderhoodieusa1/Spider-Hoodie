To preserve your hoodie, turn it inside out, wash with cold water, use mild detergent, avoid harsh bleaching agents, and air dry whenever possible. Following these steps helps protect both the fabric and graphic elements.

Storage Tips

Proper storage can significantly extend garment life. Store your hoodie in a cool, dry environment, away from direct sunlight, on a shelf rather than a thin hanger, and folded neatly when not in use. These simple habits help maintain shape and color.
